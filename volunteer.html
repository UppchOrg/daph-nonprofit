Common Questions

Do I need experience?

Not at all. We train every volunteer and match you to roles that fit your skills and schedule.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes — many of our roles are fully remote, including digital support and translation.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No minimum — we welcome one-time event volunteers as much as long-term organizers.
